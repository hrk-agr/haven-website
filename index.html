Haven is the Internet's anti-platform

The access to Internet is currently controlled by gatekeepers. This enables them to seek rent on the back of multiple businesses, often making more than what the businesses make. We believe in empowering businesses to break free from the constraints and dependencies imposed by these platforms. Say goodbye to excessive fees, opaque algorithms, and loss of control over your brand. No more being at the mercy of platforms dictating your every move.

While the existing distribution of these platforms enables new customers to discover you, it is you who should directly control their subsequent interactions. Haven empowers you to do this.
